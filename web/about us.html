About Us

At The Oxford Summer Camp, we believe in empowering our youth by providing them with the tools & skills needed to succeed. By making learning engaging and fun, our students are inspired to continue exploring the possibilities of the world around them!

Summer at our Camp – Learning, Life, Fun and Friendship

  • Summer at Oxford combines academic exploration with the energy and excitement of a summer camp.
  • Through a range of courses, master teachers strengthen participants’ academic foundations for college and career.
  • We create a powerful and unique sense of community through living and learning with diverse staff and peers from around the world.
  • Choose from our courses which encompass some of the best of the Oxford School educational experience.
;

Our Mission

Oxford Summer Camp believes that all young people, from all communities,
deserve a high-quality STEM education in order to shape their
own futures and those of the communities in which they live.
Through research and professional formation the Camp improves STEM teaching and learning
of youngsters all around the world

Our Vision

To be a premier, transformative Camp that engages in research and professional formation to advance the teaching and
learning of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
